Expand ForensicRipLogger to capture detailed track diagnostics

This commit completes the implementation of a fully verbose ripping logger.
It modifies `RipLogEvent.kt` to introduce diagnostic events covering:
- Stable overlap verification (US-001)
- Multi-pass comparison results (US-003)
- Read consistency scoring (US-004)
- Read drift detection (US-005)
- Targeted sector recovery (US-007)
- Sample alignment validation (US-018)
- Adaptive reread escalation (US-021)
- Fast-path clean ripping (US-022)

It also expands the textual output in `ForensicRipLogger` to group these
by track and logs out drive intelligence such as Cache Status (US-012),
Streaming Classification (US-013), and Preferred Read Size (US-015).
Deterministic rereads (US-002) and Atomic read sizing (US-014) have their
execution logged within the engine itself.
